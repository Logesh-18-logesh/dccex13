KERALA

Geography: Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," is a state in South India known for its lush green landscapes, backwaters, and coastal regions along the Arabian Sea.

Capital: The capital city of Kerala is Thiruvananthapuram (Trivandrum), a city known for its historic significance and beautiful beaches. Kochi (Cochin) is another major city with a rich cultural heritage.

Language: Malayalam is the official language of Kerala. The state is also known for its high literacy rate.

Culture: Kerala has a unique culture with traditional dance forms like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, classical music, and vibrant festivals like Onam. The state is famous for its Ayurvedic traditions and cuisine.
