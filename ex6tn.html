TAMIL NADU

Geography: Tamil Nadu is located in the southern part of India and is bordered by the Bay of Bengal to the east, the Indian Ocean to the south, the states of Kerala to the west, Karnataka to the northwest, and Andhra Pradesh to the north. The state has a diverse landscape, including coastal areas, fertile plains, hills, and the Western and Eastern Ghats.

Capital: The capital city of Tamil Nadu is Chennai, which is also the largest city in the state. Chennai is known for its vibrant culture, educational institutions, and as a major hub for the Tamil film industry (Kollywood).

Language: The official language of Tamil Nadu is Tamil. Tamil is one of the oldest languages in the world and has a rich literary tradition.

Culture: Tamil Nadu has a rich cultural heritage that includes classical music, dance forms like Bharatanatyam, traditional art, and literature. The state is known for its temples, and it's often referred to as the "Land of Temples." Famous temples include the Meenakshi Amman Temple in Madurai and the Brihadeeswarar Temple in Thanjavur.
