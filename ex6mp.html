MADHYA PRADESH

Geography: Madhya Pradesh is a central state of India known for its diverse topography. It is characterized by the Vindhya and Satpura mountain ranges, extensive forests, and the Narmada River flowing through its heart.

Capital: The capital of Madhya Pradesh is Bhopal, famous for its scenic beauty and lakes. Another prominent city is Indore, known for its vibrant culture and cuisine.

Language: Hindi is the official language of Madhya Pradesh, and various dialects are spoken across the state.

Culture: Madhya Pradesh is rich in cultural heritage with classical music, dance forms, and traditional art. The state is known for its historic sites, including the UNESCO-listed Khajuraho temples.
